About the Journey

My name is Mark Fugel — Jersey Mark — and for the past five years I have lived full-time on the road in Codiwomple, my 2016 Winnebago ERA built on a Mercedes-Benz Sprinter chassis. From the red rock canyons of Utah to the glaciers of Alaska, the Canadian Rockies to the Pacific Coast, I have chased sunrises, sought out solitude, and documented every mile. This site is my home base — a living map of where I have been, what I have found, and where the road leads next.
